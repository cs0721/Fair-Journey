About


The Fair journey project is dedicated to helping the disability with mobility issues to avoid the inconvenience caused by construction during their travels in Melbourne CBD. The project is committed to providing effective and efficent information to help them choose a more suitable travel plan that has reached the goal of building equality for all.

The Fair journey project timely and accurately update engineering and road information for the Melbourne CBD area through VicRoads open dataset and City of Melbourne open dataset. Customers can always search for construction information and road conditions in the vicinity of Melbourne and make travel options and plans that are more suitable for them based on the information they have.

  • Our data is provided through APIs in VicRoads and Weatherfor.us is updated hourly to maintain accuracy.

    Real-time information

  • Our system aims to provide useful information for people with long-term or temporary mobility problems. Public facilities for people with disabilities marked with reachable range.

    Designed for people with disabilities

  • The navigation system allows the user to walk from any point.

    Flexible travel plan

  • Support the pdf download file for those unfamiliar with the small screen and those planning their trip in an analog manner.

    Services for analog people
