Welcome To Fair Journey

The Fair journey project is dedicated to helping the disability with mobility issues to avoid the inconvenience caused by construction during their travels. The project is committed to providing effective and efficent information to help them choose a more suitable travel plan that has reached the goal of building equality for all.

The Fair journey project timely and accurately update engineering and road information for the Melbourne CBD area through VICroad dataset and government open dataset. Customers can always search for construction information and road conditions in the vicinity of Melbourne and make travel options and plans that are more suitable for them based on the information they have.
